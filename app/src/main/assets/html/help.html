WeatherApp Help


Introduction

WeatherApp is an Android app using the OpenWeatherMap API. You can add weather locations from around the world using a Google Map and get today's weather and a 5-day forecast for your bookmarked locations.

Adding a weather location

To add a weather location, press the add button on the bottom right corner of the home page.

A dialog box will popup and load a map. You move around the map by dragging around with one finger. or zoom in and out of the map by pinching with two fingers.

Tap anywhere on the map to select a location. Tap "OK" to bookmark the location, or tap "Cancel" to dismiss the alert dialog.

Tapping anywhere on the map will select a new location. When finished adding locations, tap the Back key to return to the Home page.

Removing a weather location

To remove a weather location, long tap on the desired location on the home page. An alert message will popup at the bottom of the screen asking you to confirm if you want to delete the location. Tap "OK" to delete the location.

If you ignore the alert message, it will disappear after 3 seconds and you have to long tap again to delete a location.

Get today's weather and 5-day forecast

To get today's weather and 5-day forecast for a location, simply tap and the desired location on the home page.

For portrait orientation, the current weather will displayed at the top of the page in white, and the 5-day forecast at 3 hour intervals will be displayed in a list at the bottom. Tap the Back key to return to the home page.

For landscape orientation, the current weather will displayed at the left side of the page in white, and the 5-day forecast at 3 hour intervals will be displayed in a list on the right. Tap the Back key to return to the home page.

Settings page

To access the Settings page, tap on the options menu on the top right corner of the action bar and select "Settings".

Tap on the "Unit of Measure" option to select between Metric or Imperial system.

Help page

To access the Help page, tap on the options menu on the top right corner of the action bar and select "Help".
